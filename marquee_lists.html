Steps to Bake a Cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and butter, then mix well.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Let it cool, then serve and enjoy!
