Understanding Headings

Headings in HTML range from <h1> to <h6>, with <h1> being the most important and <h6> the least.

Importance of Proper Structure

Using a structured hierarchy of headings helps in readability, search engine optimization, and better content organization.

Conclusion

By using different heading levels and paragraphs, you can create a well-structured webpage.
